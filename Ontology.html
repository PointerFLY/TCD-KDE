Abstract

Ireland primary school information, with county information related to these schools. Primary school has some properties, such as ethos, location, boyCount. Also it has a special property named inCounty, specifying which county it belongs to. County has basic properties such as area, adjacentTo, with hasSchools listing schools belongs to this county.
